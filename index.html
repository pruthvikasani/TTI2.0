About

Titan Company Limited (Titan), a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO), commenced its operations in 1984 under the name Titan Watches Limited. Titan is the fifth largest integrated own brand watch manufacturer in the world. Over the last three decades, Titan has expanded into underpenetrated markets and created lifestyle brands across different product categories. Titan is widely known for transforming the watch and jewellery industry in India and for shaping India's retail market by pioneering experiential retail.  Learn more

Academic institutions has the pool of knowledge, technology solutions and advanced research work that has high potential to change the way in which industries address a problem and find solutions. Though many of their research works are very useful solutions / products for companies, they find its position only in records, which benefits neither society nor industry. Titan visualized to tap this research work done by the researcher for customized areas of work most suitable / required ones for titan.

Hence Central Technology Services department at Titan company Ltd conceptualized an approach in the event format named “Technology Tune-In (TTI). TTI brings in the various research works carried out by scientist, faculty, research scholars and students from various institutions to TITAN to cater to its internal research requirements and helps in strengthening its established Industry-Academia collaboration model. TTI was first conducted during 2018 and success story is being carried forward by Launching TTI 2.0.  Know more
